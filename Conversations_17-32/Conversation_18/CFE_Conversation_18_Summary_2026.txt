o	Games: Interactive system for agency and resilience (Articles III & IV); low-stakes simulation of real-world challenges.
•	Empowering vs. Disempowering Media:
o	Empowering: Joyful, developmental, community-building; strengthens agency.
o	Disempowering: Designed to hijack attention, create division, foster helplessness; weakens agency.
•	Conscious Consumption: Active engagement and self-reflection transform media into tools for resilience, awareness, and growth.

•	Charter Role: Guides ethical AI integration:
