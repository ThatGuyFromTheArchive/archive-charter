- **Moral Triage** (Addendum II, #3) gives license to suspend nearly any principle. Without a defined decision process, this becomes a **loophole for authoritarianism** dressed up as emergency response.

#### **3. The “Conscious Actor” as Shadow Elite (FAQ Question 3)**
