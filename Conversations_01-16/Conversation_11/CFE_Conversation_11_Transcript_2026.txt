### Phase 1: The Private Sphere of Scientific Scrutiny

**"One can argue for a Private Sphere amongst scientists..."**

*   **Intensified Peer Review:** The research is shared with a wider, more diverse group of experts, specifically including those with skeptical viewpoints and those from the social sciences who can anticipate potential misinterpretations.
*   **Replication on a Massive Scale:** Before any public statement, independent teams must attempt to replicate the findings. An "inconvenient truth" requires an extraordinary burden of proof.
*   **The "Responsible Evaluation":** This is the key. Within this Private Sphere, a new team is formed, composed not just of the original scientists, but of sociologists, historians, ethicists, and communication experts. ### Phase 2: Preparing the Immune System (Pre-bunking)

The goal is not just to release the data, but to release it into a prepared environment. You cannot simply drop a live virus into an unvaccinated population. You must prepare the societal immune system.

*   **Educating the "First Responders":** This carefully crafted package is first released to journalists, educators, and community leaders with embargoes and detailed explainers. This equips the people who shape public discourse to handle the information responsibly before the public firestorm begins.
